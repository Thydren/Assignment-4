Early Life

Alan was born to Julius and Ethel Turing. Alan's father worked for the Indian Civil Service and was living in India with Ethel. They both moved back to England to raise their two sons. During Alan's early childhood, his parents would frequently go back and forth between India and England. Alan showed his genius early on and had a strong interest in math and science. By the time he was 15 he was solving advance math problems and studying calculus. By 16 he was reading Einstien's work and understanding it.
